## 11. THUMBNAIL DIRECTION (Creative Brief)

---

### CONCEPT: "The Revelation"

**LAYOUT:**
- Split composition — 60% face / 40% text
- Face on the LEFT, slightly off-center, looking directly into camera
- Text block on the RIGHT with strong hierarchy

**EXPRESSION:**
- Calm but intense. Not angry, not sad — the look of someone who has figured something out that most people haven't.
- Slight lean forward. Eyes wide and locked in.
- Authentic. No forced expressions. This is a real moment, not a reaction face.

**TEXT ON THUMBNAIL:**
- Top line (smaller, accent color): `WHAT HAPPENS TO YOU`
- Middle line (LARGE, white, bold): `WHEN YOU'RE GONE?`
- Bottom line (accent, all caps): `THE DIGITAL WILL`

**COLORS:**
- Background: Deep navy or near-black (`#0A0A1A`) — premium, serious, not clickbait
- Text: Crisp white for main line
- Accent: Gold/amber (`#D4A017`) — legacy, generational, timeless
- Optional: Subtle glowing outline on Tydro (slight warm backlight to separate from bg)

**GRAPHIC ELEMENTS:**
- Faint family photo or abstract "archive" imagery in background (blurred, ghosted at ~15% opacity)
- NO busy overlays. Let the face and text carry it.
- Small Family Relic logo/wordmark in bottom corner if branding is ready

**FONT:**
- Main line: Heavy condensed sans-serif (e.g., Impact, Bebas Neue, or Anton)
- Subline: Clean sans-serif (Montserrat Bold or similar)

**FEEL:** Equal parts grief and power. This thumbnail should make someone stop, read it twice, and feel something before they even press play.

**A/B TEST:** Run a second version where the main text reads `"THE FEAR ISN'T DYING."` — test emotional vs. question-hook performance.
