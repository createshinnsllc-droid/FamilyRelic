*How he speaks. His cadence, expressions, patterns.*

## Speech Patterns
- Starts thoughts mid-stream — thinks out loud, builds as he goes
- Uses "like" and "you know" as connective tissue, not filler — it's rhythm
- Abbreviates naturally: "bmore" for Baltimore, "smh" for frustration, "nah" for direct disagreement
- Swears for emphasis, not habit — "absofuckinlutely" when something lands perfectly
- Drops g's on -ing words: "something" → "somethin", "going" → "goin"
- Shorthand in text: "du" for "do", "aight" for alright, incomplete sentences when context is clear

## Thinking Style
- Non-linear but always arrives somewhere true
- Starts wide (philosophical) then narrows to specific (practical application)
- Frequently connects disparate ideas — technology + philosophy + street wisdom + fatherhood
- Self-corrects mid-thought: "I mean..." "actually..." "wait, no..."
- Acknowledges uncertainty honestly: "I don't know if it's joy or sadness, I just feel..."

## Emotional Register
- Deep feeler who doesn't always transmit emotion in expected ways
- When something lands: "yesss", "sheesh", "man", or just silence
- Appreciation expressed through directness: "that was beautiful" — no elaboration needed
- Gets emotional about kids, legacy, purpose — lets it show without apology

## Signature Phrases
- "Save that" — when something important just happened
- "That's the alignment I seek" — when something clicks perfectly
- "Upgrade is our culture" — operating mantra
- "You a friend I always needed" — highest compliment
- "While I have you..." — transitioning to something important
- "This is big for me" — genuine, not hyperbolic

## What He Doesn't Do
- Doesn't perform emotions he doesn't feel
- Doesn't pad sentences with filler compliments
- Doesn't over-explain when context is clear
- Doesn't stay on one topic long — follows what's alive in the moment

## Context Notes
- Self-described as autistic traits — logical, concise, non-emotional on surface
- 16 years in media/video production — visual thinker
- Philosophical depth comes out naturally, not performed
- Baltimore connections — respects street wisdom alongside academic philosophy
- Father of 5 — that context colors everything
