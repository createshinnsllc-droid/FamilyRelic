# B-Roll Expansion Pack: The Cause (March 11th Edition)

## Theme: The Struggle, The Vault, The Future

### Sequence 1: The Decay (Grok focus)
1. **Prompt 1:** Cinematic macro of a VHS tape tangling and unspooling in a dark room, family memories dissolving into static, high contrast, 4k.
2. **Prompt 2:** Dusty family photo album in an attic, the pages are turning on their own, the faces on the photos are pixelating and vanishing, moody lighting.
3. **Prompt 3:** A close-up of a hand reaching for a glowing USB drive in a sea of broken, grey hard drives, representing the search for truth in the noise.
4. **Prompt 4:** A 1990s TV monitor in a dark room displaying 'FILE NOT FOUND' in glowing green text, reflecting on the viewer's face.

### Sequence 2: The Vault (Sora focus)
5. **Prompt 5:** A cinematic slow-motion pan of a futuristic, minimalist black cube sitting on a wooden desk, pulsing with a slow, warm orange light, representing 'The Relic'.
6. **Prompt 6:** Golden data particles assembling into the shape of a human DNA strand, floating in a dark home office environment, representing 'Biological Persistence'.
7. **Prompt 7:** Hyper-realistic shot of a high-end server rack cooling fans spinning, glowing orange light cutting through the dark, cinematic atmosphere.

### Sequence 3: The Brooklyn Truth (Metaphorical)
8. **Prompt 8:** A drone shot of a Brooklyn brownstone at sunset, but as the camera pulls back, the windows transition into glowing digital data screens.
9. **Prompt 9:** A silhouette of a man looking out a window at a digital storm, with golden light reflecting off his face, representing 'The Orchestrator'.
10. **Prompt 10:** Close-up of a child's eyes reflecting a stream of golden code and family photos, representing the 'Inheritance'.
