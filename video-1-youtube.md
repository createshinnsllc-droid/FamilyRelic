## Description
What happens to everything you knew, felt, and meant to say — when you die?

Not your body. Not your soul. I mean the wisdom. The stories. The things you never found the right moment to say to the people you love.

People die every day and their families find out about bills from collection notices. Their phones are locked. Their memories are trapped behind passwords nobody knows.

This video is about that problem — and what I'm building to solve it.

Family Relic (a.k.a. The Digital Will) is an open-source framework for preserving who you actually are — your voice, your reasoning, your stories — in a way your family can actually access.

Not a company. Not a subscription. A community building something that belongs to all of us.

I've been on YouTube since 2011. This is the first video I've made that actually matters.

If this resonates — if you've lost someone and wished you had more of them — drop a comment answering this:

**"If someone you loved died tomorrow, what would you wish they'd left you?"**

That answer is what we're building.
