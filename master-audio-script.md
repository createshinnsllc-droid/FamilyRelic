### SEGMENT 6: THE COMMUNITY (Day 6 - The Movement)
"I’m not interested in building this alone. I’m looking for a community of like minds—fathers, builders, and thinkers who are tired of being silenced by time. If you see the same gap I see, you’re not just a follower. You’re a builder of the Relic."

### SEGMENT 7: THE CALL (Day 7 - Phase 1)
